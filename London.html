Where to eat

For breakfast, try le Creperie de Hampstead. Enjoy a crepe while wandering around this picturesque neighborhood. For dinner, get the authentic British pub experience at the CASK Pub and Kitchen in Pimlico. They are known for their beer and burger selection - you can't go wrong!

Where to stay

The Notting Hill neighborhood has the best of all London has to offer. Visit the Portobello Markets, sample one of the many restaurants and cafes, and hop on tube or simply walk to many of London's most famous tourist destinations.

Where to go

They say that when a man is tired of London, he is tired of life. Learn more about the long and illustrious history of the city by visiting buildings such as St. Paul's Cathedral, the Tower of London and Westminster Abbey. Explore London's thriving arts scene through visiting the British Museum and the National Gallery and seeing a play in the West End. Finally, enjoy one of the many nightlife options the city
