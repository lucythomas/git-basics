Where to eat

Enjoy a delicious breakfast at "Passe Compose" on Rue Roy Est in the Plateau Mont Royal neighbrhood. In a quiet spot among lovely traditional homes you can pretend you're in France for a morning, looking out the window and sipping your coffee.

Where to stay

Start in the bustling downtown area and do some shopping on Rue St. Catherine. Explore more residential neighborhoods such as Le Plateau Mont-Royal and Mile End. Don't forget to get lost in the cobblestone streets of Vieux Montreal.

Where to go

If it's warm enough, explore the Montreal Botanical Garden and the Parc Jean-Drapeau. In Vieux Montreal, go to one of the city's oldest churches, Notre-Dame-de-Bon-Secours Chapel. Visit one of Montreal's many museums, such as the Museum of Fine Arts, and experience the nightlife on Rue St. Denis.
